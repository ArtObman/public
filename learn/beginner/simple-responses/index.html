Actions As Output#

Earlier, we just made the bot respond with some static text. However, often we want our custom command to behave differently depending on input. This is where template actions come in.

Template actions are a way to dynamically change the output depending on various things, such as the user who triggered the command, the arguments passed to the command, or even the current time.
